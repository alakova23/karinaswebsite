Cities to Explore
Prague. In the previous few years, Prague has established itself as one of the most touristic and visited cities in the World. Moreover, this is happening not only because the architecture of Prague is one of the most unique in Europe, but also because the Place Branding program of is detailed and tactical, which helps the city develop and prosper. For example, a large number of international students give Prague the status of an international city, which for businessmen is one of the key qualities in choosing a place for investment.

According to the analysis of the previous year, Lisbon has grown significantly in terms of investment and tourists. The city administration is making every effort to preserve the uniqueness of the city, which is noticeable in the blocks and streets that retain their appearance for many years
